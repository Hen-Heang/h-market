# DRMS - B2B Trading Platform

## Overview

DRMS is a B2B trading platform connecting **Partners** and **Merchants** to streamline business operations and enable faster, more efficient trading.

## Platform Roles

### Partners (formerly Distributors)
Partners are suppliers who can:
- Manage stores
- Manage products
- Manage orders
- Connect with multiple merchants

### Merchants (formerly Retailers)
Merchants are buyers who can:
- Discover partners
- Place orders
- Track transactions
- Manage business relationships

## Key Benefits

- **Reduced Time**: Streamlined processes minimize the time needed for business operations
- **Reduced Complexity**: Simplified workflows make trading easier and more efficient
- **Faster Trading**: Quick order placement and tracking enable rapid business transactions
- **Better Connections**: Platform facilitates discovery and relationship building between Partners and Merchants
